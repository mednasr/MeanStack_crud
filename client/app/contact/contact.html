Technical Test

Using a RESTful micro-service architecture, create a web page with :

1. A list of contact (firstname | lastname | age)
2. A contact can be added to the list
3. A contact can be remove from the list
4. Once the contact is selected, you can edit his detail (including the address)
5. The address must have at least the street #, street name and a city
6. The city must be chosen from a list receive by the address service

You must have 3 services (app):

1. Front-end (UI)
2. Contact
3. Address

- The UI service must call both the Contact and Address service to render the page.
- The Contact service must call the Address service to get the address detail of a contact.
- Contact and Address must use a RESTful architecture.
- The address service must provide a list of cities.
